crème brûlée

Crème brûlée is a simple dessert with profound flavor. Consisting of a few staple ingredients, a compotent cheif transforms sugar, cream, and egg, into a delecate dessert. Caramalization is the final step to create a crisp shell of melted sugar, providing a much needed contrast in both color and texture. It can be enjoyed as is, or topped with a variety of tart fruits to further emphasis the sweet flavors.
